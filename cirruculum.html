Introduction to Coding

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of coding. Topics covered include basic programming concepts, syntax, and problem-solving skills. Students will learn to write code in languages such as Python, JavaScript, and Scratch.

Art and Design Basics

Explore the world of art and design in this introductory course. Students will learn about different artistic styles, techniques, and mediums. Hands-on projects will allow students to unleash their creativity and express themselves through visual arts.

Mathematics Mastery

Master essential mathematical concepts and skills with this comprehensive course. Topics covered include arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and more. Engaging activities and problem-solving exercises will help students develop a deep understanding of mathematical principles.
